Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2, 2025

Introduction

Remembrance ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. The Remembrance app is designed to operate entirely on your device.

Photos and Content

All photos and memories you add to the app are stored locally on your device. We do not upload, store, or have access to any of your photos or personal content on our servers.

Subscription Information

If you purchase a subscription, your payment is processed through Apple's In-App Purchase system. We do not collect or store your payment information. Apple handles all payment processing according to their privacy policy.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Photos you import into the app
  • App settings and preferences
  • Subscription status (managed by Apple)

Data Security

Your photos and data remain on your device and are protected by your device's security features. We recommend using device passcode protection and enabling device encryption for additional security.

Third-Party Services

We use Apple's StoreKit framework for subscription management. Apple's privacy policy applies to any information collected through their services.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed to be used by adults to commemorate loved ones.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@remembranceapp.com

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information. You can delete the app at any time to remove all data from your device.
